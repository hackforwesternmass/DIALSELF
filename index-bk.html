Welcome to DIAL/SELF Youth and Community Services

We are a community-based non-profit agency that has been serving the youth and communities of Western MA since 1977. We provide a wide array of services that foster youth empowerment and community service. Over the course of the last three decades, more than 30,000 area youth have been served by the agency in Franklin and Hampshire Counties and the North Quabbin Region.

DIAL/SELF programs include:

Youth Services      

  • TeenLine
  • Safe Place
  • Step Program
  • North Quabbin Office
  • Teen Housing

Community Service Partnerships

  • ACT Volunteer Center
  • Youth Serve AmeriCorps
  • RISE
  • Youth Services Roundtable

Our Mission

DIAL/SELF provides a broad range of services that foster youth empowerment by: meeting basic needs, creating opportunities for youth, and advocating for youth interests in the communities we serve.

Our Philosophy

We believe that all youth possess unique strengths, which are enhanced by community support and collaboration, and that all youth should have the right to exercise choice in their own lives.
