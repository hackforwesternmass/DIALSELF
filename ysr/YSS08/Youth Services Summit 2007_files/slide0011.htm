RISE modeled after 'Communities in Schools'
• Communities in Schools (CIS) is the largest and most successful organization in the country focused on integrating schools and communities.  Begun in 1977, CIS is now serving over 3,000 school sites in 28 states.  More than 2 million students annually benefit from CIS programs.
•
• CIS programs help students successfully learn, stay in school, and better prepare for life.  A local CIS accomplishes these goals by bringing community resources into schools, and by making schools a resource to the community.
