RISE-Coordinated Services:
•Recruiting and training volunteers
•Identifying college students who seek internships in the youth development field and bringing those students into the Middle School to provide enrichment activities
•Collaborating with Franklin Medical Center to develop health and wellness programs
•Coordinating with community agencies to offer services to Middle School students (e.g. Big Brothers Big Sisters, Community Action!, The Franklin Hampshire Career Center, The Interfaith Council, The Youth Commission).
•Involving the business community in career initiatives
•Working with local arts-based initiatives to bring arts/music to the school
•Responding to any innovative, creative opportunities dreamed up by students, parents, teachers, or community members!
