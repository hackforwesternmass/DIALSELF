Pre-Forum Trainings
12/06/06 20:33


Pre-forum trainings will be held on four consecutive Wednesdays at the Greenfield Community Youth Center, starting on January 3rd and continuing on January 10, 17, and 24th.

All pre-forum trainings will be held between, 5-7 p.m., and dinner will be provided for participants. The pre-forum trainings are open to all youth between 14-21 years old. Certificate of Completions will be given to everyone who attends at least three trainings. For more information contact Jennifer Johnson at (413) 774-7054.
