The 14th Annual Badminton Bash
March 29, 2015

 

 

Online registration is open for the 2015 Badminton Bash. Register here!

 

The Bash will be held in the Forslund Gym at the Northfield Mount Hermon School in Gill, MA on March 29, 2015

 

Event runs from 10:00am - 3:30pm.

Tournament times Choose which time is best for you.
*Early Birdie - 10:00am - 12:30pm
*Sunday Brunch - 1:00 - 3:30pm

Format:
2 distinct tournaments with trophies awarded to the top two teams. Each team requires 3 players.

Pledges:  This is a fundraiser and all players are expected to collect pledges/donations of at least $50 in order to participate. We will be offering prizes to players who meet certain pledge benchmarks. Please note that T-shirts will only be given to players who raise at least $50.

Pledges & Prizes:
$50 + Commemorative t-shirt
$150 + Commemorative hooded sweatshirt & commemorative t-shirt

Early Registration:  Players who submit their registration and pledges by March 13, 2015 will have their prizes waiting for them on the day of the event.

REGISTER EARLY to guarantee your team's place. Players who submit registration and pledges early can receive t-shirts before the event or have their prizes waiting for them on the day of the event.

Trophies:
Trophies will be awarded to the top two teams in each tournament. In addition, Wild Card trophies will be awarded at the discretion of the Bash officials to players or teams for demonstrating extraordinary effort, best costuming, “Most Improved,” “Most Enthusiastic” and other noteworthy accomplishments.

Raffle: There will also be an opportunity to buy tickets for raffles with great prizes from local businesses.
